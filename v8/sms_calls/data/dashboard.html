Flow

SMS and call jobs are created in Firestore. The device polls every few seconds, claims pending work, enforces allowed numbers and quotas, and then writes logs back.

Gateway Guide

Rails writes jobs here

sim_module/settings/sms_jobs/{autoId}
sim_module/settings/call_jobs/{autoId}

Rails manages policy here

sim_module/settings
sim_module/settings/allowed_numbers/{safePhoneNumber}

Device writes results here

sim_module/settings/sms_logs/{autoId}
sim_module/settings/call_logs/{autoId}

Test flow

  1. Add the target number in Numbers and keep it enabled.
  2. Queue an SMS or call job with status pending.
  3. Watch Serial for JOB lines if jobLogs is enabled.
  4. Read final queue status and the matching log entry.
